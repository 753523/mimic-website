Collaborative research

MIMIC is a freely available dataset developed by the MIT Lab for Computational Physiology, comprising deidentified health data associated with >40,000 critical care patients. It includes demographics, vital signs, laboratory tests, medications, and more.

Patient care

Our aim is to make better use of routinely collected data to improve healthcare. Outline of some of the current issues in healthcare and how data may help.
